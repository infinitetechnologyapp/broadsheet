What is SchoolNova?
Complete School Management System
A cloud-based platform that manages student results, attendance, broadsheets and academic records — all in one place, accessible from any device.
The Problem We Solve
No More Manual Result Computing
Schools spend weeks computing results by hand every term. SchoolNova eliminates all manual work — positions, grades and broadsheets are generated instantly and accurately.
Smart Attendance
Morning + Afternoon Tracking
Track AM and PM sessions daily. Automatic holiday exclusion, weekly analytics, per-student summaries and percentage calculations — all done for you.
Role Based Access
Admin, Form Teacher, Subject Teacher
Every user sees only what is relevant to their role. Admins see everything. Form teachers manage their class. Subject teachers manage their subjects only.
